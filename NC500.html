NC500 Overview

~850 km around Scotland's north coast. RV support from a friend. Day count (5, 6, or 7) to be decided after the April simulation tour. Plan assumes ~130–170 km/day depending on day count.

  • Start/finish: Inverness
  • Elevation: ~8,000–10,000 m total (variable by route)
  • Key climbs: Bealach na Bà, Applecross, Kylesku, multiple coastal headlands
  • Target pace: Z2 endurance with Z3 on climbs — NC500 is a multi-day event, not a race

Atlanta training context

  • Jun–Aug: Zwift indoors — Atlanta summer heat makes outdoor riding impractical
  • Sep onward: Stone Mountain Park — ~12.3 mi loop, ~849 ft elevation
  • April block: North Georgia mountains (Three Gap / Dahlonega-Suches area) — the single most important training block

Zone 2 on variable terrain

Zone 2 compliance on rolling terrain like Stone Mountain means targeting Z2 on flats and descents while accepting Z3 on climbs. Rigid HR ceiling enforcement isn't appropriate on variable terrain. Power is the primary metric; HR is a secondary fatigue check.
